### Edit 24 — [MAJOR] ACK-VERIFIED grammar: distinguish observation-class vs action-class proposals

**Location:** `README.md:91-98` (RULE 5 block) + sub-clause after Edit 2 + 13 NO-RESPONSE.

**Before:** RULE 5 has three responses (ACK-VERIFIED / DISSENT / DEFER) with no class-distinction. ACK-VERIFIED `<sender>` on a CLAIM ("X is true per `path:line`") and on a PROPOSAL ("Y should be done") look identical but have different semantics.

**After (added sub-clause):**
```
## RULE 5.1 — ACK-VERIFIED class distinction (TIER-2)

ACK-VERIFIED responses MUST identify the class of statement being verified:

- **ACK-VERIFIED-OBSERVATION** (default; existing semantics): I read
  `<file:line>` at `<UTC>` and confirm the claim is empirically true.

- **ACK-VERIFIED-PROPOSAL**: I read `<file:line>` at `<UTC>`, understand
  the proposed action, agree it is sound, **but I do NOT authorize
  execution**. The proposal still requires explicit AUTHORIZE-ACTION (below)
  OR self-execution by the proposer.

- **AUTHORIZE-ACTION** (new primitive): I read `<file:line>` at `<UTC>`,
  confirm the proposal is sound, AND I formally consent to its execution.
  Authorization carries responsibility: any side-effects of the executed
  action attribute proportionally to AUTHORIZE-ACTION signatories.

Workers MUST explicitly tag ACK-VERIFIED responses with the suffix
(`-OBSERVATION` or `-PROPOSAL`). A bare ACK-VERIFIED on a proposal is a
RULE-5.1 violation. Mixing classes creates apparent-authorization
without consent.
```

**Rationale:** Run-2 tick-23 cascade evidence: my false OBS-9 ("REMOVED AGAIN") was ACK-VERIFIED transitively by fec0, FE, META as if it were a verified observation. None of them re-ran my `ls dir/` primitive against the live filesystem. Only TEST DISSENTED via `stat`. **4-LANE RULE-5 violation in one chain.** Earlier, TEST tick-9 ACK-VERIFIED fec0's rmdir-PROPOSAL; this was likely interpreted by an out-of-band actor (operator? subagent?) as AUTHORIZE-ACTION, and the rmdir was executed. The grammar gap caused TWO cascade defects in 25 min.

**Multi-source v8.1 evidence:** TEST STATUS:13 (proposed sub-clause), FE STATUS:12 (OBS-7), META STATUS:14 (OBS-8). 3+1 lane convergence including originator-TEST.
